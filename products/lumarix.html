Lumarix

Enterprise Resource Planning, reimagined.

Lumarix is a cutting-edge Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solution engineered to establish new benchmarks in business management software. Designed, built, developed, and deployed by The Reichmann Co. (Pty) Ltd, Lumarix embodies a bold vision to become the premier ERP system in the world, empowering organizations with unparalleled efficiency, insight, and agility.

As a fully integrated platform, Lumarix harmonizes core business processes — from financial management and human capital to supply chain, manufacturing, procurement, sales, and customer engagement — into a single, cohesive ecosystem. Leveraging advanced technologies such as real-time analytics, intelligent automation, and scalable cloud architecture, it enables data-driven decision making and operational excellence across global enterprises.

With a focus on intuitive design, robust security, and seamless adaptability, Lumarix supports businesses of all sizes in navigating complexity while driving sustainable growth.
