Lumarix

Enterprise Resource Planning, reimagined.

Lumarix is a cutting-edge Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solution engineered to establish new benchmarks in business management software. Designed, built, developed, and deployed by The Reichmann Co. (Pty) Ltd, Lumarix embodies a bold vision to become the premier ERP system in the world, empowering organizations with unparalleled efficiency, insight, and agility.

As a fully integrated platform, Lumarix harmonizes core business processes, from financial management and human capital to supply chain, manufacturing, procurement, sales, and customer engagement, into a single, cohesive ecosystem. Leveraging advanced technologies such as real-time analytics, intelligent automation, and scalable cloud architecture, it enables data-driven decision making and operational excellence across global enterprises.

With a focus on intuitive design, robust security, and seamless adaptability, Lumarix supports businesses of all sizes in navigating complexity while driving sustainable growth.

The Lumarix spec sheet

Modern accounting and ERP, designed and built in South Africa. Here's what's in the box on day one, and where we're going.

At launch

  • General Ledger with full double-entry
  • Customer invoicing & quotes
  • VAT module with VAT201 export
  • Bank reconciliation
  • Core financial reports (TB, P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow, Aged Debtors & Creditors)

Built different

  • True cloud-native. No installs, no sync headaches
  • Real-time multi-user collaboration
  • AI-assisted transaction categorisation
  • AI-assisted bank reconciliation
  • Sage Pastel migration tool included

Trust & compliance

  • POPIA-compliant by design
  • EU data centres today, with a South African region planned
  • Immutable audit trail on every transaction
  • Role-based access for owners, admins, accountants, bookkeepers and viewers
  • Encryption at rest and in transit

Scales with you

  • Plans for small, medium and enterprise businesses
  • Multiple companies under one organisation
  • Multi-currency-ready architecture
  • Custom chart of accounts with SA industry templates
  • 30-day free trial. Per-user pricing with feature tiers

Coming next

Phase 2 adds full Inventory, Payroll (with EMP201 and IRP5), direct SARS e-filing, and live SA bank feeds. Phase 3 brings a native mobile companion and a partner API for marketplace integrations. More to follow as we build.

Inventory, done properly

Inventory lands in Phase 2, right after the core accounting modules go live. Built for everything from a single shop floor to multi-warehouse manufacturing.

Items & structure

  • Stock, non-stock, service, and kit items
  • Bill of Materials for light manufacturing
  • Multi-level categories and tags
  • Item variants (size, colour, and more)
  • Item images, gallery, and datasheet PDFs
  • Per-item VAT rates (standard, zero-rated, exempt)

Stock tracking

  • Multi-warehouse with location-level stock
  • Serial number tracking (toggle per item)
  • Batch and lot tracking with expiry dates
  • Multiple units of measure with conversion
  • Barcode generation and scanning (EAN, UPC, Code 128, QR)
  • Real-time perpetual accounting on Weighted Average Cost

Buying & selling

  • Multiple suppliers per item with preferred and per-supplier pricing
  • Purchase Order, Goods Receipt, Supplier Invoice workflow
  • Multiple price lists with customer-tier overrides
  • Customer-specific price overrides
  • Stock reservations from quotes and sales orders
  • Backorders and partial fulfillment

Models that fit you

  • Drop shipping
  • Consignment stock (in and out)
  • Configurable negative stock policy
  • Re-order point suggestions per location
  • Granular permissions for stock, costs, and POs
  • CSV catalog import and Sage Pastel migration
