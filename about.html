About The Reichmann Co.

The Reichmann Co. (Pty) Ltd is a South Africa–based technology company specialising in custom software development, from native mobile apps to enterprise SaaS platforms.

We design, build, and ship software across every major platform: iOS, Android, web, and desktop. Our work spans bespoke client engagements and our own portfolio of SaaS products, each built to the same uncompromising standard.

Founded on the belief that great software starts with great engineering, we partner with founders, businesses, and operators to turn ideas into products that ship. Fast, polished, and built to scale.

Headquartered in South Africa, we work with teams worldwide — primary hours are Mon–Thu 08:00–16:00 and Fri 08:00–12:00 SAST (UTC+2), with async collaboration outside of those hours.
